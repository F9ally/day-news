## Active Funds See Surging Inflows But Can They Beat the Market?

**Active Funds See Outsized Inflows**

Active ETFs have recently seen a resurgence in inflows, with 34% of ETF money going towards actively managed ETFs. This trend suggests that investors are regaining confidence in active management and are willing to pay a premium for professional guidance.

**Reasons for Active Funds' Resurgence**

* Wall Street is regaining its reputation with recent developments and active managers getting positive PR.
* New ETF launches are offering lower fees and greater liquidity, attracting investors who prefer active funds.
* Market volatility creates more opportunities for active managers to outperform.

**Performance of Active vs Passive Funds**

While actively managed funds can be very successful, most investors would likely be better off investing in passive index funds. Studies show that investing in a passive index fund consistently outperforms actively managed funds over the long term.
