# Cipher Strike: Production Goes On Strike A small independent film crew in Kentucky has gone on strike due to an attempt to organize for a union contract under the IATSE union. The crew, working on the low-budget movie "Cipher," began shooting on May 12 and are currently filming under a Directors Guild of America and SAG-AFTRA contract. They are seeking health plan and pension benefits as the primary reason for their desire to collectively bargain. The union gave the production company until Wednesday to respond but did not hear back, leading to the crew members staging a "recognition strike."

## China's Tianwen-2 Mission to Return Samples from Mysterious Asteroid Kamo'oalewa **Overview:** - China's Tianwen-2 spacecraft successfully launched on May 28, aiming to retrieve samples from the enigmatic asteroid Kamo'oalewa. - It will spend ten years exploring this asteroid, searching for clues about its origin and formation. **Key Points:** * Kamo'oalewa is a unique quasi-satellite that follows an oscillating orbit around Earth. * China's mission is the first of its kind, following the successful sample returns from the Moon and near-Earth asteroid Itokawa. * The asteroid is expected to reveal insights into its origin, composition, and evolution. * The mission will also investigate the phenomenon of active asteroids, a class of objects that resemble comets but reside in the asteroid belt.
