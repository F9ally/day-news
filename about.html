About Day2Day News

Day2Day News is your go-to source for bite-sized summaries of the most important events from around the world. News summaries are updated daily at 6:30am GMT. Please also bear in mind that this site is still under development. Features such as audio, personalized and past summaries aren't fully functional.

Curated AI, our goal is to help you stay informed in under 5 minutes each day. Despite testing, AI can make mistakes, so always verify the source. Links to the entire news feeds are always available, below the paragraphs.
