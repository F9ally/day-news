About Day2Day News

Day2Day News is your go-to source for quick, reliable summaries of the most important events happening each day around the world. This is bite sized news without the bulk.

Curated daily by AI, our goal is to help you stay informed in under 5 minutes a day. We go through testing to insure information is accurate and trustworthy. However AI can make mistakes, so always verify the source. Links to the entire news feeds are always available, simply click on the title to get redirected to the source.

Day2Day News is a project by Astronaut, an independent website and app developer.
